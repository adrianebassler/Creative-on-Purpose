STEP 1 · WHAT YOU HAVE

What proof or content can you use right now?

Customer proof
Reviews, testimonials, case studies, client results. Real words from real customers. A quote, a video, a DM screenshot, anything showing it worked for someone else.
Founder story
Your origin story or why you built this. The moment you realized the problem, what you tried, what you figured out. Can be written or on camera.
Before and after visuals
Transformation photos, progress shots, side-by-sides. Can be physical change, lifestyle change, business results, anything that shows the shift.
UGC video
Customer filmed this on their phone. Raw, real footage of them talking about their experience or showing the product in action. Doesn't need to be perfect.
Organic content that's working
Posts or Reels getting strong saves, shares, or comments. Anything your audience is already stopping for and engaging with without you spending money on it.
Press or media mentions
Features, awards, podcast shoutouts, editor picks, best-of lists. Any third party mention that says someone outside your brand checked and agreed you're worth attention.
Active offer or promotion
A discount, bonus, launch price, founding member deal, first visit incentive, anything with a deadline or reason to act now instead of later.
STEP 2 · BUSINESS TYPE

What are you selling?

Service
You work with clients one-on-one or in groups. Coaching, consulting, design, photography, anything where you're selling your expertise, your time, or a done-for-you service.
Digital product
Courses, templates, memberships, ebooks, digital downloads. Anything someone buys once and accesses on their own without needing you live on the other end.
Local business
Brick and mortar, service area business, anything where people need to show up to a physical location or you come to them. Restaurants, salons, gyms, home services, local retail.
Ecommerce
Physical products you ship. Skincare, apparel, home goods, supplements, anything tangible that shows up in a box at someone's door.
STEP 3 · WHERE'S THE BREAKDOWN?

Where are you stuck right now?

Haven't launched ads yet
You're building from scratch or getting ready to run your first round of ads. You need to know what to create first and what to layer in next.
Ads are running but no one's stopping
You're spending money but your ads aren't getting attention. People are scrolling past. Low engagement, low clicks, not much happening at all.
Getting attention but not getting sales
People are seeing your ads, maybe even clicking, but they're not buying. Something is breaking their confidence before they commit.
It worked before and now it doesn't
Your ads were performing and now they're not. Results dropped off. Your warm audience has seen everything and stopped responding.
